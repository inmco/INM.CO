Before making a quality design, INM.CO do a process where the process can make a good picture in our opinion. There are several stages to make your design look as good as possible. Here are the steps:

A Creative Brief is a document used to explain to you (the client) about the project to be carried out, including the objectives, target market, design price, etc. This document also explains the types of designs that will be applied to your business, as well as the technical specifications you will need in the future.

The Creative Brief will be used as a reference for INM.CO in executing the project according to your wishes and expectations.

01 / CREATIVE BRIEF

After the brief process is complete, the client is required to deposit first so that the design process in INM.CO can run well. The deposit that must be paid is 50% of the previously agreed design price.

02 / DEPOSIT 50%

Research here is a plan used to execute a brief that you have previously given. This plan includes the selection of design and data collection, as well as the analysis of the data to be carried out by INM.CO.

03 / RESEARCH

After the research is completed, it will proceed to the sketch and design stage. Usually INM.CO will provide sketch options up to 2-3 options after that continue with the design process, the design here will use the results of existing research of course.

04 / SKETCH & DESIGN

A design presentation is a presentation that presents a design that we have designed before. In terms of concept to color selection, we will explain here.

05 / PRESENTATION

Furthermore, the final presentation, the final presentation will be delivered more fully than the presentation that has been made before.

08 / FINAL PRESENTATION

Repayment payment, after the initial deposit of 50%, then in the next payment there will be an additional 50% of the previous payment which is still less

09 / FINAL PAYMENT

After fixing everything, INM.CO will send a complete design soft file, while the soft file given is usually a vector file, .jpg, .png, .pdf, etc.

10 / FILES DILEVERY

Responding to the designs we have made

06 / FEEDBACK

Selection of designs to be used as final projects.
